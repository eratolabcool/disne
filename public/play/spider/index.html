How to Play Spider Solitaire

Spider is a classic single-player card game. Play unlimited games free on your phone, desktop, or in full screen. No download, no registration required.

Objective

Remove all cards by building complete same-suit sequences from King down to Ace. Completed sequences are automatically removed.

Rules

Move cards between columns in descending order. In 1-suit mode any descending sequence works; in harder modes only same-suit sequences move as a group. Deal 10 new cards from stock when stuck.

Strategies to Win

Build same-suit sequences early. Keep empty columns for temporary storage. Avoid mixing suits. Plan ahead to expose face-down cards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play Spider for free?

Yes. Spider on Solitaire Arena is completely free. No download, no registration, just open and play.

Is Spider solvable?

Nearly all Spider deals are solvable with the right strategy. Use our hint system to find the best moves.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. Spider works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The game adapts to your screen size.

What is the best score in Spider?

Scores are calculated by adding the number of seconds to the number of moves. Lower scores are better. Check the leaderboard for top players.

What is the difference between Turn 1 and Turn 3?

Turn 1 deals one card at a time from the stock pile. Turn 3 deals three cards at once, making the game harder. Solitaire Arena supports both modes.
